Eligibility Rules for Internal Assessment

To be eligible for appearing in the internal assessment, a student must satisfy all the following conditions. The student’s attendance should be greater than > 75%. The total number of assignments submitted must be less than < 5. In addition, the internal assessment score should be less than or equal to ≤ 40.
